Privacy Policy for DOCMAN

Last updated: July 2026

DOCMAN ("the app") is a personal document management app for Android. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Summary

  • DOCMAN does not have its own servers and does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data to the developer or any third party.
  • Your documents, PIN, and biometric data stay on your device.
  • Google Drive access is used only when you choose to import a file, and only for that purpose.
  • The app contains no ads and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Documents you store in the app

All documents (PDFs, images, and other files) you add to DOCMAN are stored locally in the app's private storage on your device. They are never uploaded to any server operated by the developer. They remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.

PIN and biometric lock

If you enable the app lock, your PIN is stored on your device only, as a one-way cryptographic hash — the app never has access to your actual PIN in readable form, and it is never sent anywhere.

If you enable fingerprint or face unlock, this is handled entirely by Android's built-in biometric system. DOCMAN never sees, stores, or has access to your biometric data (fingerprint image, face scan, etc.) — the app only receives a yes/no confirmation from the operating system that you were authenticated.

Locked documents and your PIN hash are also excluded from Android's automatic backup system, so they cannot leave your device via cloud backup or device transfer.

Camera

The "Take Photo" option opens your device's own Camera app to take a picture, which is then added to your documents. DOCMAN does not access the camera directly and does not process or store any images beyond what you explicitly capture or select.

Google Drive import (optional)

If you choose to import a file from Google Drive, DOCMAN opens a Google sign-in page in your browser. You choose which file to share; DOCMAN then downloads a copy of only that file into the app's local storage. DOCMAN does not access any other files in your Drive, does not store your Google account credentials, and does not retain the temporary access token used for that one download.

Permissions the app uses

  • Internet — used only to download a file you explicitly selected from Google Drive.
  • Biometric hardware — used only to unlock the app if you enable this feature; handled by Android, not the app itself.

DOCMAN does not request access to your contacts, location, microphone, SMS, or call data.

Data sharing and third parties

DOCMAN does not share your data with third parties. The app does not include advertising or third-party analytics.

Data deletion

You can delete individual documents at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data, including documents, settings, and the PIN hash.

Children's privacy

DOCMAN is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same page with a new "Last updated" date.
